About the exchange

The New Zealand Switzerland Exchange is a non profit organisation which allows Year 12 students of French to experience life and school in French speaking Switzerland for a period of eight weeks at a relatively low cost.

  • New Zealand families host a Swiss student in August/September.
  • Our New Zealand students travel to Switzerland in December/January to stay with their host sister/brother’s family.
  • The exchange culminates in a trip to Paris.
